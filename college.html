ABOUT D.N. PATEL COE

P.S.G.V.P. Mandal’s D. N. Patel College of Engineering, established in 1983 by the esteemed Sahakar Maharshi Shri Annasaheb P. K. Patil, was founded with the vision of empowering the younger generation through advanced technological education. The institution is committed to nurturing future technocrats by instilling discipline, competence, and strong character—equipping them to thrive in a rapidly evolving global landscape. Under the dynamic leadership of Shri Bapusaheb Dipakbhai Patil, President of PSGVP Mandal, the college has seen continual progress. It holds the distinction of being the first A-grade college under North Maharashtra University, Jalgaon, and is currently affiliated with Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University (Dr. B.A.T.U), Lonere. The institution boasts a rich legacy of excellence and has earned recognition from various educational bodies. It is accredited by the AICTE and NBA, while the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), Bangalore, has awarded the college a 'B' grade in its first assessment cycle.

The college has a progressive management team led by Shri. Bapusaheb Dipakbhai Patil, President, PSGVP Mandal. This college was the first A-grade college in North Maharashtra University, Jalgaon, and is presently affiliated with Dr.B.A.T.U University, Lonere.

The college has a glorious past with recognition from different educational agencies. AICTE has offered NBA accreditation and NAAC, Bangalore has conferred a ‘B’ grade in the 1st cycle.

The college provides instructions for various undergraduate courses in the streams of Civil, Computer, E & TC, Electrical, Instrumentation, and Mechanical engineering. The teaching faculty consists of 54 learned faculty members and experts in their own subject, discharging their duties with a sense of dedication and devotion.
